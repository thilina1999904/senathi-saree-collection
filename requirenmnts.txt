1. Project Name: Senathi Saree Collection Web App 

2. Technology Stack: MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) 

3. Key Modules:

Authentication: User login & Admin login.
Product Management: Add, Edit, Delete, View Sarees.
Cart & Order: Adding to cart, Checkout, Order placement.
Payment: Online (Card) or Manual (Bank Slip Upload).
Admin Panel: Managing orders and stock.
